Real Questions

The Questions You're Already Thinking.

Isn't this just MLM? +
I get why you're asking. The word "MLM" carries a lot of baggage — and most of it's earned. House parties. Pestering family members. Buying product you don't need to hit a quota. That's not this.

This is a referral-based model with an established American wellness company. Customers buy directly from the company. No inventory. No minimums. No "selling" in the way you're imagining. I built seven figures here by building genuine relationships and sharing something I actually use. That's it.
How much time does this actually take? +
It depends on the outcome you want. Women building a modest supplemental income ($1K–$3K/month) typically invest 5–10 hours per week, focused and intentional. Women building at the level I have — 20–30 hours, especially in the early years. The investment front-loads. The income becomes residual.

The honest answer: this rewards people who have the capacity and willingness to be consistent for 12–36 months. It's not a quick payday. It's a compounding asset. If that model fits your life, the conversation is worth having.
I've tried things like this before and it didn't work. +
That's the most common thing I hear from the women who go on to do the best here. They were burned, skeptical, and almost didn't reach out. Most of the time, what didn't work was: a company that collapsed, a mentor who wasn't invested in their success, a model that required techniques that felt wrong, or a product they didn't actually believe in.

What I offer is different in all four of those ways. A proven company, personal mentorship, no-pressure approach, and products I've used for years. If you want to tell me what didn't work last time, I'll tell you honestly whether this is different or not.
What does it cost to get started? +
There is no cost to get started. No storefront, no employees, no inventory to buy. You start with a conversation — and if it's a fit, you're in. We'll walk through everything together so there are no surprises.
Can I really do this if I don't have a big social media following? +
Yes. Some of the strongest builders in my community started with a hundred followers or less. This business is built on relationships and trust — not follower counts. We teach you how to find the right people, build genuine connection, and have conversations that lead to results. Follower count is a vanity metric. Relationship depth is what moves this.
