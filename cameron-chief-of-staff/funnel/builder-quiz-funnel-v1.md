### QUESTION 1: What's your real situation right now?
*Be honest — this is where it starts.*

- A) I'm comfortable. Things are fine. I'm just casually curious what this is. **(0 points)**
- B) I could use more money, but I'm not in any rush. I'll figure it out eventually. **(1 point)**
- C) I'm underpaid, stretched thin, or carrying financial stress that keeps me up at night. I need this to work. **(3 points)**
- D) I'm done. I'm either drowning in debt, capped out at a job that doesn't value me, or I've hit a wall and I refuse to stay here. I need a real change — now. **(4 points)**

### QUESTION 2: How do you move when something feels right?
*This is the one that sorts the room.*

- A) I research for weeks, make a pros/cons list, and usually talk myself out of it. **(0 points)**
- B) I take my time. I like to think it over for a while before I do anything. **(1 point)**
- C) I do my due diligence, but once I decide, I move. I don't need hand-holding. **(3 points)**
- D) When I know, I know. I take action fast, figure out the details as I go, and I hate waiting. I'm already thinking about what's next. **(4 points)**

### QUESTION 3: What would make this feel worth it beyond the money?
*Pick the one that's actually true for you.*

- A) Honestly, just the extra income. I don't really need anything else from it. **(1 point)**
- B) Flexibility — I want to work on my own schedule without answering to anyone. **(2 points)**
- C) Purpose. I want to be doing something that actually matters, not just clocking in. I want to feel lit up by my work again. **(3 points)**
- D) All of it — the income AND being part of something bigger than me. I want to grow alongside women who are driven, ambitious, and going somewhere. I'm hungry for that community. **(4 points)**

### QUESTION 4: How do you feel about where your career or income is right now?
*Don't sugarcoat it.*

- A) It's good. I'm satisfied with where I am professionally. **(0 points)**
- B) It's okay, but I feel a little stuck. I'm not sure what the next step looks like. **(2 points)**
- C) I'm underpaid for what I bring to the table and I know it. There's a ceiling here and I can see it. **(3 points)**
- D) I'm over it. I've outgrown it, I resent it, or I left it entirely. I'm done letting someone else decide what I'm worth. **(4 points)**

### QUESTION 5: What does your inner circle look like right now?
*Be real.*

- A) I have a solid support system. My people are great and I'm not really looking for more. **(0 points)**
- B) It's fine. I have friends and family around but we don't really talk about goals or ambition. **(1 point)**
- C) I feel a little isolated, honestly. I want to be around women who are building something — I just haven't found my people yet. **(3 points)**
- D) I'm craving it badly. I want a real community of ambitious women who push each other, celebrate each other, and actually show up. I'm tired of being the "driven one" in every room. **(4 points)**
